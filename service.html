Self-awareness paves the way for great achievements and deeper connections.

I offer support, counselling, and resources to individuals and communities, working towards enhanced well-being, addressing social issues, and improving overall quality of life.

This is achieved through a personalised approach, empowering individuals to overcome challenges, fostering resilience, and building a foundation for positive change.

Gain a deeper understanding of yourself, those around you, and your circumstances

As a social worker, my role is to assist you in understanding challenges, overcoming obstacles, and fostering positive transformation in your well-being and relationships.

Counselling Services

INDIVIDUAL: 9-12 SESSIONS
GROUPS: 6 WEEKS

In these sessions, my role is to guide and support you in gaining a deeper understanding of your emotional triggers.

Through individual sessions tailored to your specific needs or group sessions spanning six weeks, we will work collaboratively to explore and navigate the challenges you're facing.

Together, we'll develop coping strategies, foster emotional well-being, and empower you to navigate your unique journey towards positive change.

Advocacy Services

PERSONAL ADVOCACY AND COMMUNITY OUTREACH

In the realm of advocacy, I act as your dedicated advocate, ensuring your rights are protected and your needs are met on an individual level.

Additionally, through community outreach initiatives, I work to address systemic issues and promote positive change within the broader community.
